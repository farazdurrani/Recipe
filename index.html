    • Tuna Sandwich

    Ingredients

    • Tuna
    • Pepper N Salt
    • Lettuce
    • Tomato
    • Bread
    • Mayonnaise

    Directions

    1. Drain out all the water from the Tuna can and put Tuna into a plate
    2. Put 2 spoons of Mayonnaise and mix it
    3. Put some salt and pepper
    4. Mix pieces of Tomatoes and cut lettuce and mix into it.
    5. Spread it on a bread.
    6. Sandwich is ready!!
